<div class="research-core">
  <h1>Research Focus</h1>
  <p>I work on understanding how atoms and electrons behave under extremely strong laser fields. My research focuses on modeling and simulating ionization dynamics using analytical and numerical techniques.</p>

  <div class="focus-block">
    <h2>Current Themes</h2>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Above-Threshold Ionization (ATI):</strong> Photoelectron spectra beyond the ionization threshold.</li>
      <li><strong>Saddle Point Approximation:</strong> Quantum interference and complex time trajectories.</li>
      <li><strong>Strong Field Approximation (SFA):</strong> Modeling electron-laser interactions analytically.</li>
      <li><strong>Twisted Laser Pulses:</strong> Impact of orbital angular momentum on ionization.</li>
      <li><strong>Non-Dipole Effects:</strong> Exploring relativistic light-matter dynamics.</li>
    </ul>
    <img src="/assets/img/pmd.png" alt="PMD with Bessel Pulse" class="research-img"/>
    <p class="img-caption">Photoelectron momentum distribution generated using a Bessel beam.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="focus-block">
    <h2>Now Working On</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Electron emission under twisted few-cycle laser fields</li>
      <li>Non-dipole ATI peak shifts and interference effects</li>
      <li>Saddle-point dynamics vs. full-path integral analysis</li>
      <li>AI-assisted clustering of quantum trajectories</li>
    </ul>
    <img src="/assets/img/saddlepoint.png" alt="Saddle Point Paths" class="research-img"/>
